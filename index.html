Iron Giant

The selection has been replaced with original sounds. The lazer sounds were all generated via modular synthesizer, while the metal sounds were processed from original field recordings I made at the recycling warehouse where I work. Various sources include a vintage spotlight controls, sheetmetal, the servo motors from an old VCR and a pile of Imac cases. Finishing touches were added using vintage cavalry and explosion sounds that came with the Dave Yewall book I am reading. The vintage tape fidelity of these samples fit well with the subject matter.

Demonstrates: Sound Design, Modular Synthesis, Field Recording, Use of Sound Banks, mixing







Terrior of Tea

This passage was recorded with an SM7 and processed lightly with multiband compression, a gate to remove extrainious background noise and some manual touches to clean up sibiliance and pops. Background music quickly sequenced in numerology with the Zebra2 synthesizer and mixed in with side compression.

Demonstrates: Podcast Vocal recording, Music Composition, Editing and De-essing
